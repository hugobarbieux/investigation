There are several financial instruments used in this area for the current budget period (2014-2020), but the main one sits outside the EU budget and is the 11th European Development Fund (EDF - €30.5 billion), with contributions from EU countries. The EDF has its own procedures. We invite you to read more in the EDF-dedicated web page in DG DEVCO website.

Please note however that there are other instruments under the EU budget 2014-2020 which are also used for development cooperation, such as the Development Cooperation Instrument (€19.7 billion), the Instrument contributing to Stability and Peace (€2.3 billion) or the European Instrument for Democracy & Human Rights (€1.3 billion). You can find a summary in this DEVCO web page and their specific regulations in Eur-lex.

You can also get a good overview in the "2018 Annual Report on the implementation of the European Union's for financing external actions in 2017", in the associated Staff working document or in the summaries of EU legislation, under the "development" chapter.
